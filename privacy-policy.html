Privacy Policy for tidy.

Effective date: 3/19/2026

tidy. respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how tidy. handles information when you use the app.

Information We Access

tidy. requests access to your photo library only so you can review and manage screenshots stored in your Photos library.

The app may also request permission to send notifications if you choose to enable reminder notifications.

How Information Is Used

Photo library access is used only to:

  • Identify screenshots in your photo library
  • Let you review screenshots
  • Let you save screenshots within the app
  • Let you send screenshots to the dustpan
  • Let you delete screenshots you choose

Notification access is used only to:

  • Send optional reminder notifications if enabled by the user

Data Collection

tidy. does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data on external servers.

All screenshot management happens on your device using Apple system frameworks.

Third-Party Services

tidy. does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking services.

Data Sharing

tidy. does not share your data with the developer or with third parties.

Data Retention

The app may store simple preferences on your device, such as:

  • Screenshots you marked as saved
  • Screenshots placed in the dustpan
  • Reminder notification settings

This information stays on your device unless removed by you or deleted with the app.

Photo Deletion

When you choose to delete screenshots, they are removed using Apple Photos APIs and may first be placed in the “Recently Deleted” album in the Photos app according to Apple’s system behavior.

Children’s Privacy

tidy. does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • Deny or revoke photo library access in iOS Settings
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app and its locally stored preferences at any time

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

The tidy. team at support.tidy@gmail.com
