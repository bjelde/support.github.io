FAQ

How does tidy. work?

tidy. scans your photo library to identify screenshots, then lets you quickly review, keep, or remove them in a simple, focused experience.

How does screenshot deletion work?

When you choose to delete a screenshot, tidy. requests permission and removes it using Apple's standard Photos framework.

Where do deleted screenshots go?

Deleted screenshots are moved to the "Recently Deleted" album in the Photos app, where they remain for a period before being permanently removed.

How can I get help?

You can contact support at any time using the email link above. We aim to respond promptly and resolve issues quickly.

Does tidy. access or store my data?

tidy. only accesses your photo library to help you review and manage screenshots you choose. Your photos are never stored, uploaded, or shared.
